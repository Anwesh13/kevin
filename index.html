Hello Everybody, i am

Kevin S. Shah, MD, FACC, FHFSA

Dr Kevin Shah is a practicing cardiologist with subspecialty training in treating patients with heart failure, including those who need ventricular assist devices (VADs) or heart transplants. Dr. Shah is an academic cardiologist, spending his time balancing clinical care, research, and training students, residents, and fellows.

  • Salt Lake City, Utah

About Myself

Dr. Shah is a member of multiple professional societies including the American College of Cardiology and Heart Failure Society of America. He has previously served on the American Society of Transplantation Thoracic and Critical Care Community of Practice Executive Committee as well as the Heart Failure Society of America Scientific Publications Committee. His research has been published in high impact journals including the Journal of College of Cardiology and Circulation. His research interests include advanced heart failure, disparities, and reducing the burden of heart disease in the South Asian community. He is board-certified in Internal Medicine, Cardiovascular Medicine, Heart Failure & Transplant Cardiology, and Echocardiography
